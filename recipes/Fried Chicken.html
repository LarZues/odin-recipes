Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) Chicken, cut into pieces
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour for coating
  • 2 Quarts vegetable oil for frying

    • Directions

    1. In large skillet heat oil over medium heat
    2. Salt and Pepper chicken pieces to taste
    3. Coat chicken in flour
    4. Place chicken pieces in skillet and fry on medium heat until one side is golden brown
    5. Brown chicken on other sde until chicken no longer pink inside
    6. Drain on paper towel and serve
